1. Order Confirmation

All orders are confirmed through a phone call or WhatsApp within 24 hours. Please ensure your contact details are accurate to avoid delays.

2. Delivery Information

Within Jodhpur: ₹30 flat delivery charge. Outside Jodhpur (All India): ₹70 flat delivery charge. We aim to deliver within 5-7 business days from the date of confirmation.

Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for Jodhpur only. Outside Jodhpur, we accept advance payments via UPI. Payment details will be shared upon order confirmation.

Each rakhi is handcrafted and carefully quality-checked before dispatch. Pictures of the exact products will be shared with you before shipping to ensure full transparency.
